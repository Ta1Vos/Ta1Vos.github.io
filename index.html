About Me

Hi there! I'm Tim Vos and I'm 16 years old! I live in the Netherlands, Zoetermeer.
I am currently following the study of Software Developer on MBO level 4 on the ROC Mondriaan. After this study I've planned to follow the study Game Developer. In this way I will walk into the world of programming with experience about web development.
As of now I am mostly learning Front-End development, but next year I will start to learn back-end programming and will learn advanced Front-End.

I myself, am a creative person in the aspect of code and can think of smaller details that fit with websites and games.

Although I am great with the functionality of a website, I unfortunately do not have the ultimate skill of design.

    Projects

    Higher Lower

    Higher lower is my first project at ROC Mondriaan, which is a gambling game.

    With just little access to the internet I've managed to assemble a simple-designed, yet high functional gambling game.

    I've made this gambling game with pure CSS, HTML & JavaScript. I didn't have the knowledge over Bootstrap or any other libraries.
    I have challenged myself to make an advanced game from its beginning, and with the use of the web and the helpful teacher, I have been able to create a game with such functionality as a started.

    Alcatraz Escape Room

    At the end of period 1, we had been challenged to create a digital escaperoom in 1 week by our teachers.

    This is the first project where I have officially worked in a team, although upon reflection upon this project, I've found some aspects I had to improve in.

    Teamwork: In this project there has been a lack of communication and task division inbetween the teammembers, me included. We did have our own parts we worked on.

    Teamwork has been an issue for me for a much longer time, but I wish to improve that in upcoming projects & other experiences, so it won't be a struggle for the future.

    TicTacToe

    TicTacToe is the third project I have made. I've made it throughout my second period of school.

    This is, once again, a solo project I have made myself.

    One thing I'm proud of for this project, is that I have made an algorithm you can play against (called the computer). Its practically impossible to beat it as it covers all ways you could win. The game would result in a tie until the player would make a mistake.

    (If you'd wish to try it out, you will have to change the difficulty in the settings at the menu)
    The Path of The Dabloons (Custom project)

    The path of Dabloons is an RNG-based game (highly inspired from the dabloon memes) I have made at the end of period 2. This is a two-week project, which would be called 'battle of the JavaScript'. For this project we had to think of a concept and make it have a lot of functionality with JS.

    This project had to be solo made and pretty much anything was allowed as long as you could explain the code.

    For this project I have challenged myself a lot, thinking of many solutions and possibilities I could make with JavaScript. I discovered new things like: promises, executing functions through strings, and more!

    Perhaps I'll work further on this one day, but for now I still have large projects for school.

    ROC Mondriaan clothing web catalogue

    This is the 5th project I have made (in period 3). This is the first project where I officially worked as a team with classmates.

    For this project we had to make a clothing website, where everyone made 1 regular page and 1 clothing category.

    This is the first project where I've learned about the JavaScript fetch (getting information from databases). This can be seen in the clothing categories.

    I have made the contact page and the shoes category in this project.
    Colour Madness

    This is another project I have worked on in period 3. This was for an elective part of my study, called Game Production.

    For this I had to investigate on how to create a game, while also investigating what to make. It made me think no second about a concept I've wanted to make reality for a long time.
    And yet, here it is!

    This is the first time I have interacted a lot with a JavaScript library.
    I have used Phaser in order to make my concept reality.

    When looking at certain topics/concepts, I can find myself loads and loads of motivation and inspiration to the point I just can't stop thinking about adding more and more. Just add that little asset, oh wait! not to forget that other very puzzling asset!

    For most of my school projects I have managed to keep challenging myself to keep adding more until I reach the deadline, of which I had fun too.

    ROC Mondriaan dashboards

    This is the 6th project for period 4, which is also the last project of year one. In this team I work, once again, officially in a team.

    Like the last project, we had to make 1 regular page and 1 dashboard (which shows information about certain things).

    On this project, I have made the chatbot and the kassabon (receipt) dashboard. In this period, new things weren't introduced, it was mostly repeating everything what we've learned in the past 3 periods. In this project I did make use of ChartJS.
